About Us

An organisation that deals with problems in Cyberspace and related to cyberspace. Spreading awareness on safety in cyberspace.

Awareness campaigns for all. Tying up with security related products and organisations. Assist the victims for legal recourse. Assist the victims for technical recourse. Assist the victims in emotional recourse like counselling, medical assistance etc.

Contribute in creating a future that’s safer, cleaner and ethical

The awareness programs can be classified as:

  • for children
  • for teachers
  • for parents
  • for working people
  • for entrepreneurs
  • for Social workers or social entrepreneurs

Pure Elegance & Perfect Training

The 12-year-plus Avanzo history in bite-sized chunks has done, more than 600 workshops for school children, more than 200 for teachers and parents addressed working people and entrepreneurs through more than 100 sessions in tie up with TMA / CII / KITCO etc.

Handled more than 3000 cases related to social issues with children in spot first toll free number on cyber helpline launched in the year 2010 – 1800-266-8001. WhatsApp helpline active and working since 2015 – 81297711. Handled more than 200 calls per week on the toll free number handling hundreds of help requests on WhatsApp helpline since 2025 every day.

Worked in alliance with organisations like Rotary and Lions for spreading awareness. Worked with various NGOs for helping the victims of cyber crimes. Worked with law houses to give legal aid to cyber victims. Worked on papers and projects to improve the awareness on cyberspace security. Prepared material to be circulated and distributed in-house. Prepared courseware for children for Std 1 to 12 on cyber safety. Trained hundreds of trainers on cyber safety. Partnered with various organisations to spread awareness – like NMC/ SG/ Bank of Baroda etc.

Conducted various competitions like – essay writing, colouring, poster making etc for students of different age groups. Have addressed almost 1 lakh students through the curated curriculum based program in schools – CAP. Our Avanzo programmes include CAP projects, seminars, workshops, and other community events. 600+ seminars / workshops in various schools, colleges and social forums on cyber awareness. The participants for each of these events will be an average of 150 members. Addressed more than 1,00,000 students in the last 7 years through the program CAP.
